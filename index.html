Tom, Ko Yu Ting

Biography

Tom, Ko Yu Ting received the M.Sc. degree in electrical engineering and the M.Phil. degree in computer science and engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2007 and 2010 respectively. He then received the Ph.D. degree in computer science and engineering from the same school in 2014. Throughtout his postgraduate studies, he was supervised by Professor Brian Mak. He is currently working as a researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab in Hong Kong His research interests include speech recognition and machine learning.
